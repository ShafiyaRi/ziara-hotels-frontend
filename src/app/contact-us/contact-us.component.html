About Ziara Hotels

Welcome to Ziara Hotels, where luxury meets comfort. Our mission is to provide an unparalleled experience for our guests through exceptional service and premium amenities. Nestled in the heart of breathtaking landscapes, Ziara Hotels offers a perfect blend of modern elegance and timeless charm.

History of Ziara Hotels

Established in 1975, Ziara Hotels has grown from a single boutique hotel to a renowned chain of luxury accommodations. Our founder, Ms.Shafiya, envisioned creating a haven for travelers seeking a unique blend of relaxation and sophistication. Over the years, we have stayed true to this vision, continuously evolving to meet the needs of our discerning guests.
